# Womens Hair Loss — DirectCare AI

Single-page landing for DirectCare AI's women's hair regrowth program.

Built in the same style as [sexual-health-mocha.vercel.app](https://sexual-health-mocha.vercel.app/)
and `mens-hair-loss.vercel.app`: dark video hero, product cluster cards,
interactive ingredient explorer, science triad with SVG diagrams, 3-question
quiz, pricing, testimonials, FAQ, and a final CTA — all driving to the
Tellescope hair-loss intake form.

## Protocols featured

1. **Topical Women & Men** — Minoxidil 5% + Fluocinolone 0.01% + Tretinoin 0.005%
2. **Topical Women & Men** — Dutasteride 0.1% + Minoxidil 5% + Tretinoin 0.025%
3. **Oral Women** — Spironolactone 50/100 mg
4. **Oral Women & Men** — Low-Dose Oral Minoxidil 2.5 mg
5. **Oral Women Combo** — Minoxidil 1mg + Biotin 25mg + Nutrients 100mg
6. **Supplement** — High-Dose Biotin 10,000 mcg
7. **Shampoo** — Ketoconazole 2%
